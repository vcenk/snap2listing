# Snap2Listing Pricing Strategy

## Cost Analysis (Internal - Not shown to users)

### Per Listing Costs:
- Text generation (title, description, tags): ~$0.01
- Image generation (4 images avg): ~$0.30 ($0.075 per image)
- Video generation: ~$0.30 per video
- Upscaling (optional): ~$0.05 per image

**Average cost per listing:** $0.35 - $0.70 depending on usage
